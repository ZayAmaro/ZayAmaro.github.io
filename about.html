About This Blog

By Zay Amaro

Who I Am

My name is Zay Amaro, and this blog is a space where I explore what it means to live out Christian faith in everyday life. My experiences in sports, discipline, and personal growth have shaped how I view faith beyond traditional settings.

Why This Blog Exists

Faith Beyond the Field is about applying faith outside of church walls—on the field, in the gym, at work, and in daily decisions. Through reflection and conversation, this blog aims to encourage others to live intentionally and faithfully in all areas of life.

What You’ll Find Here

You’ll find blog posts focused on faith, discipline, leadership, and personal development, often connected to sports and real-life experiences.
